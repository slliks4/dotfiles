### 🛠️ Quick Setup Tips

1. **Set Okular as default PDF viewer**:

   ```bash
   xdg-mime default okularApplication_pdf.desktop application/pdf
   ```

2. **Enable Dark Mode in KDE**:

   * Go to `System Settings > Appearance > Global Theme`
   * Pick `Breeze Dark` or download more themes

3. **Customize your shortcuts for study flow**:

   * `System Settings > Shortcuts > Custom Shortcuts`

4. **Restore session on login**:

   * `System Settings > Startup and Shutdown > Desktop Session`
   * Enable: **Restore previous session**
